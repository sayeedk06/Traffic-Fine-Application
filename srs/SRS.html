1.Introduction

1.4 Product Scope

The application “Traffic Police” is intended mainly towards government officials who are in charge of upholding the traffic rules in our streets. Normally to case a fine against a car which is parked illegally a traffic police would have to get the cars registration number in the number plate manually and then issue a fine to the driver of the car against his/her license. But this is very time consuming. So the application aims to automate the whole process, making it smoother and easier.


Goal:
  • A mobile application that takes picture of a number plate and assigns a certain amount of fine to its owner.
Benefits:
  • A cross platform application.
  • The admins being able to keep track of the officer’s activity through his case history.
  • Automating a very complicated process at the current system.
